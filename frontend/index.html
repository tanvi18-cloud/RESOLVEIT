Welcome to ResolveIt

ResolveIt is your trusted platform for fair, transparent, and expert-guided dispute resolution. Register your case, share evidence, and let our mediation panel help you reach a solution.

How It Works

  • Register as a user and submit your dispute case.
  • Upload supporting documents, images, or audio as proof.
  • Our admin team will review your case and notify the opposite party.
  • Nominate witnesses and let the mediation panel guide the process.
  • Track your case status and receive a formal agreement upon resolution.
